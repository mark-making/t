--- layout: team-member title: Gaby Bastyra ---

Natural History film-making runs in her veins. She has produced several BBC Natural Worlds including My Life as a Turkey, The Unnatural History of London and blue chip series Earth A New Wild for National Geographic and PBS. She started her career as winner of Best Newcomer Award at Wildscreen and has gone on to win several Wildscreen and Jackson Hole awards including Best Series & the Golden Panda.

She’s already clogged Talesmith’s systems with mind bendingly good ideas and we can’t wait to turn them into fully fledged productions!
