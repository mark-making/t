--- layout: team-member title: Dom Walter ---

He has been at the heart of Talesmith’s team from the beginning and has quickly grown through the ranks to become indispensible. Dom is a talented AP with a rich scientific background, a flare for creating treatments and a passion for natural history. Dom has worked on BBC1’s Forces of Nature (2016), ITV1’s Secret Life of Growing Up (2016), Mygrations for National Geographic (2015), The Hunt for BBC1 (2016) and Star Gazing Live for BBC2 (2015).
