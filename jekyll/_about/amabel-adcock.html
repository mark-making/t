--- layout: team-member title: Amabel Adcock --- With a background in zoology Amabel has over 15 years experience making science and natural history factual docs, factual-entertainment series, drama-docs and immersive entertainment.  Amabel has a proven track record sourcing compelling contributors, building strong narratives, coordinating multi-camera shoots and working with high profile presenters as well as extensive worldwide filming experience. She has a deep understanding of new and emerging technologies having worked with leading VFX houses, studios and CG artists creating 2D, IMAX, 3D, VR and MR content.  Most notable productions include Hold the World VR with David Attenborough, Natural History Museum Alive 3D with David Attenborough and Galapagos 3D with David Attenborough.
