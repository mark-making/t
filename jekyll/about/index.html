Talesmith

About us

We are EMMY and BAFTA winning producers of ambitious multi-platform content for broadcast, museums, cinema, VR and digital.

With glossy natural history, science and adventure documentary close to our heart, Talesmith was founded by four-time Emmy-winning filmmaker Martin Williams with the aim to deliver a new kind of specialist factual content, specifically designed to appeal to the modern generation.

Our team has a long and glittering track record in producing award-winning content fronted by some of the biggest names in television. With a focus on new technologies, very high production value, and a multi-platform output we are firmly focused on the international market and are always looking for ways to innovate and modernise.
